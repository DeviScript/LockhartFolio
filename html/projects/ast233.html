AST 233 | Astronomy

Learning Objectives

  1. Cultivate an understanding of science's role in our daily lives.
  2. Ignite excitement about scientific discoveries and their historical context.
  3. Show how science evolves to explain the 'how' of phenomena.
  4. Demonstrate that key scientific principles underpin a wide array of phenomena.
  5. Enhance skills in reading and evaluating scientific media.
  6. Reflect on the cosmic events that permit our existence on Earth.

Final Project | AST 233 Astronomy

This final project is the culmination of our study in AST 233, where we explored the impactful role of science in everyday life and the excitement of astronomical discovery. We delved into how scientific understanding evolves and its capacity to explain a vast range of phenomena. Through critical evaluation of scientific media, this project synthesizes these insights, illustrating the profound implications of astronomy on our perception of Earth and our existence within the cosmos.
