Acknowledgment

In the EBD 482 course, I was honored to be one of the 155 individuals selected worldwide to explore the intricacies of Global Entrepreneurship. This course has allowed me to rigorously investigate the multifaceted challenges and opportunities that such ventures entail.

Collaborating with an international team, I contributed to developing a business proposal addressing a challenge set forth by a Polish organization, honing my ability to work effectively across diverse cultures and time zones. My participation in this course was not just academic; it was a practical immersion into global teamwork, both in-person and virtually, that culminated in a comprehensive reflection on the course activities through journaling, solidifying my understanding and appreciation of global business dynamics.
